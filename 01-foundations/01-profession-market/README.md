## 1. What is an AI Engineer?
There is a lot of confusion. Let's clear it up.

### The Spectrum
- **Data Scientist:** Focuses on *Research* & *Math*. Builds models, analyzes data, minimizes loss functions. Output: A model weight file or a report.
- **ML Engineer:** Focuses on *Ops* & *Training Infrastructure*. Scaling training clusters, CUDA kernels, serving infrastructure. Output: A training pipeline.
- **AI Engineer (You):** Focuses on *Product* & *Systems*. Assembling models, RAG pipelines, Agents, and APIs into a working product. Output: A shipping application.

> **Mental Model:**  
> The Data Scientist invents the engine.  
> The ML Engineer builds the factory to mass-produce the engine.  
> The **AI Engineer** designs the car, puts the engine in it, and ensures it drives safely on the highway.

## 2. Market Reality (2025)
The "Prompt Engineer" role is dead. It was a feature, not a job.
Companies realized that writing prompts is just one part of the software engineering lifecycle.

### What Companies Actually Hierarchy
1.  **Software Engineering Fundamentals:** Can you write clean code? Can you deploy it?
2.  **System Design:** Can you architect a RAG pipeline that doesn't hallucinate?
3.  **Model Intuition:** Do you know when to use GPT-4o vs Llama-3-70b?
4.  **AI Specifics:** Embeddings, Vector DBs, Agents, Tool calling.

### The "T-Shaped" Engineer
- **Broad Knowledge:** Backend, Frontend, DevOps, Database.
- **Deep Knowledge:** LLM Orchestration, RAG, Eval.

## 3. Creating Value & Salary
AI Engineers are currently the highest-paid band of Software Engineering because they bridge the gap between "Cool Demo" and "Business Value".

- **Junior:** Scripts, simple RAG, calling APIs.
- **Senior:** Reliable Agents, Evals, Latency Optimization, Cost Control.
- **Principal:** Custom Model Fine-tuning strategies, Enterprise Architecture, "Buy vs Build" decisions.
